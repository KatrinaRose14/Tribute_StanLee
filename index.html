Stan Lee

“With great power comes great responsibility.”

Stan Lee (December 28, 1922 - November 12, 2018) was an American comic book writer best known for his work with Marvel Comics. Throughout his life, he created 200 characters.

Some of his most iconic characters are:

Spider Man

Ant Man

The Hulk

Iron Man

Thor

Loki

Black Panther

Black Widow

Doctor Strange

Groot
